About Northern Numbers

Free, accurate calculators built specifically for Canadians.

Northern Numbers was built on a simple idea: Canadians deserve financial calculators that actually reflect Canadian rules — not American formulas with a maple leaf slapped on top.

Most calculators online use US tax brackets, US mortgage compounding, and US contribution limits. That's a problem when you're trying to figure out your actual TFSA room, your real mortgage payment under Canadian semi-annual compounding, or how much CMHC insurance will cost you.

Every calculator on this site is built and verified against official Canadian sources — the CRA, CMHC, and Bank of Canada — and updated as rules change each year.

There's no sign-up required and no paywall. Your calculations stay on your device and are never sent to our servers. We take privacy seriously and will always be transparent about what we collect.

Have a calculator you'd like to see built? Let us know.

Trusted Canadian Sources

Every calculator on Northern Numbers is built and verified against official Canadian sources. We update our data annually when CRA and government rates are published.

  • 🇨🇦 Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) — Tax brackets, TFSA limits, RRSP limits, FHSA rules, RESP/CESG, CPP and EI rates
  • 🏠 Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC) — Mortgage insurance premiums, stress test rates, amortization rules
  • 🏦 Bank of Canada — Inflation targets, historical rate data, monetary policy
  • 📋 Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) — OAS, GIS, CPP benefit amounts, EI premium rates
  • 🏛 Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI) — Mortgage stress test qualifying rates
